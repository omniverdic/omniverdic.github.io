Reaction time test

This test has two parts, one of which is a visual part where you will be shown five different colors on which your reaction time will be tested five times on each color.

There is also an audial part where three different sounds will be played and your reaction time will be tested five times per sound.

Please use a computer or laptop with a connected mouse for this test.
